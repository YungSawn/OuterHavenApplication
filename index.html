Petition for the Rank of Officer

Submitted in Honour & Humility
🛡

To our esteemed Guild Master and the assembled officers of this brotherhood — I write not out of ambition, but out of devotion. For as long as I have carried this guild's banner, I have done so without condition, without falter, and without question. It is in that same spirit that I now put forth this petition, trusting it to be judged on the merit of my deeds rather than the boldness of my words.

🌅Ever PresentOnline at dawn, at dusk, and through the deep hours of the night when the realms grow quiet.
🤝Steadfast AidNo dungeon request refused, no crafting favour ignored, no guildmate left stranded.
👑Sworn LoyaltyMy allegiance to our Guild Master is not earned — it is given, wholly and without condition.

My presence in these halls has been constant. When members call for aid — whether it be a struggling newcomer lost in their first raid, a veteran seeking a capable partner for a gruelling world boss, or simply a soul in need of advice — I answer. Not occasionally. Not when convenient. Always. I have made it my purpose to ensure that no member of this guild ever feels unseen or unsupported.

Yet beyond service to our members, there is a deeper truth I would lay before you: my loyalty to our Guild Master is not the loyalty of convenience or calculation. It is absolute. Their vision for this guild is my vision. Their commands are my commands. Should they decree we charge into the maw of oblivion itself, I would be the first through the gate — and the last to leave.

I do not seek the rank of Officer for power or prestige. I seek it so that I may serve more effectively — so that the trust our members place in this guild, and the trust I place in our leader, may be honoured with the full weight of my commitment. I am already an officer in deed. I humbly ask only to be one in title.

Peak / Klaw
Loyal Servant of Outer Haven
